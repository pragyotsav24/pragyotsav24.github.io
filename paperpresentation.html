Paper Presentation

Step into the world of ideas at our Paper Presentation Event! Dive into thought-provoking discussions, exchange insights, and broaden your horizons. It is where curiosity meets discovery, and learning becomes an adventure. Join us in presenting your innovative papers!

Rules:
• Participants must only register through our Google form.
• A team can contain about 1 to 3 members.
• Topic is open to a field of your choice and is to be submitted along with the abstract 5 days prior during
registration.
• Shortlisted team will be sent a mail, after which they will have to send their PowerPoint.
• The team's PowerPoint should not be longer than 10 slides. References must be mentioned.
• The best paper will be awarded to the most practical idea amongst the others.
• College ID card is mandatory.

Registration Fee: Rs.150
