Robo Line Follower

Start your engines and blaze a trail at the dynamic Robo Line Follower Challenge. Prepare to witness remarkable robotic feats as they navigate a predefined path with precision and creativity. Let's kick off the excitement with a high-voltage start line that'll leave sparks flying!
Rules:
1. Teams limited to three members, with individual participation allowed.
2. Pre-registration mandatory, one entry per team.
3. Participants must arrive 30 minutes before and stay till the end.
4. Event has two rounds, with the second having two subdivisions.
5. Rule violations lead to team disqualification.
6. Judges' decision is final.

Registration Fee:

  • For Non Members- Rs.200
  • For Members- Rs.300
