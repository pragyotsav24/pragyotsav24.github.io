Robo Line Follower

Start your engines and blaze a trail at the dynamic Robo Line Follower Challenge. Prepare to witness remarkable robotic feats as they navigate a predefined path with precision and creativity. Let's kick off the excitement with a high-voltage start line that'll leave sparks flying!
Rules:
• Teams can have up to four members, solo participants are also allowed.
• The competition comprises of two rounds on a white background with a black track.
• The track width is set at 3cm and the robots must operate independently.
Pre-made kits are strictly prohibited; violators will be disqualified.
Teams can be interdisciplinary and from different colleges, but each team must build its robot.
• Any number of sensors but max 4 actuators for movement is allowed
Touching robots during runs incurs penalties and the best-designed robot receives bonus points.
The organizers' decision is final.

Registration Fee:

  • For Non Members- Rs.200
  • For Members- Rs.300
