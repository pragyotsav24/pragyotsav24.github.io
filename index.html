OUR COLLEGE

Easwari Engineering College


Easwari Engineering College is an autonomous institution (as per the 2019 regulations) that was instituted in the academic year of 1996-1997, with the approval of the government of Tamil Nadu and AICTE, New Delhi. As per government regulations for affiliation, originally, the college was affiliated with the University of Madras. Subsequently, the college was taken over by Anna University, Chennai, in 2002. Pursuing the philosophy of continuous learning, the institution and the students aim for credibility in academic sessions, also encouraging them to develop their interpersonal and intrapersonal skills and attitudes to approach life with confidence.
