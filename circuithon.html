Circuithon

Step into the dynamic world of Circuithon, where your inner inventor ignites, challenges thrill, and innovation knows no bounds! Don't miss out-Spark your brilliance and seize the victory!
Rules:
• Teams are limited to a maximum of 2 members.
• Pre-registration is mandatory.
• One entry per team is allowed.
• Participants must arrive 30 minutes before and remain until the event ends.
• The event consists of three rounds.
• Volunteers will monitor for rule violations.
• The judge's decision is final.
• Incase of damage to any of your robot, the organising committee will not be responsible.

Registration Fee: Rs.100
